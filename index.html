About Me

Hello! I'm Aymane Dakdaki, an enthusiastic and dedicated Cybersecurity professional. As a young professional, I offer complete flexibility and a commitment to fully invest my time and energy into making significant contributions to a team.

My passion for IT, particularly cybersecurity and network engineering, is demonstrated by my continuous self-learning. I actively enhance my skills through online courses, in-depth study of technical books, and extensive hands-on experience with platforms like TryHackMe and HackTheBox, where I've successfully navigated numerous practical lab scenarios. This has helped me build a strong foundation in troubleshooting, system optimization, network engineering, web development, and core security principles.

During my internship at RYD MediaTech, I gained valuable hands-on experience in web development, cybersecurity, and IT support. I collaborated within a multidisciplinary team to develop and secure web applications, conducted penetration testing to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities, and provided crucial technical assistance. A notable project was the "AIGC Challenge," an AI competition where I contributed to building and securing the event's website, alongside providing technical support for design and video editing.

I am eager to apply my adaptability and enthusiasm to a challenging role, collaborate with seasoned professionals, contribute to meaningful projects, and grow alongside a team to achieve collective success. I am confident that my commitment will allow me to make an immediate and positive impact.
